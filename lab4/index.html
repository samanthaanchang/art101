Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task (Creating a Digital Artwork on iPad)

    
					
  1. Turn on your iPad.
    2. 
					
  2. Open the art application, specifically open Procreate by tapping on it.
    3. 
					
  3. In the top right corner, press the "+" symbol, and hit "Square", which has the preset of 2048 x 2048px.
    4. 
					
  4. On the top right, click on the paintbrush tool and select "6B Pencil" under the "Sketching" category.
    5. 
					
  5. Now, start dragging your finger over the blank canvas to create an original artwork.
    6. 
					
  6. If you need to erase some of the artwork, use the eraser button located on the top right of the screen, and tap the area to erase it.
    7. 
					
  7. After completing your artwork, go to the top left of the screen and click the wrench icon.
    8. 
					
  8. Then, click on the "Share" button.
    9. 
					
  9. Click on "PNG" under Share Image.
    10. 
					
  10. Finally, press "Save Image" and the artwork will be saved to your device's gallery.
